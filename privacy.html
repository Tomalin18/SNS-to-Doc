Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

SNS-to-Doc is a browser extension that allows users to send social media posts (from X/Twitter and Threads) directly to Discord via webhooks, optionally using AI services such as OpenAI or Anthropic for content processing.

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All configuration data — including your Discord webhook URLs, API keys, and custom instructions — are stored only in your browser’s local storage.

Data Sharing

No data is shared with the developer or any third party. The only outbound connections are direct API calls to services you explicitly configure (such as OpenAI or Anthropic) and to your own Discord webhook URL.

Third-Party Services

When you enable AI processing, your content may be sent securely via HTTPS to the API provider you choose (OpenAI or Anthropic). Please refer to their respective privacy policies:

  • OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Discord Privacy Policy

Storage and Security

All settings and API keys are securely stored in Chrome’s storage.local area and never leave your device.

Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this policy, please contact the developer via GitHub.
